ABOUT ME

Playing baker in her childhood home and trying out every sweet recipe the Betty Crocker cookbook had to offer, Kristen created... a MESS usually.

But on the rare occasion she used baking soda instead of baking powder and real butter instead of margarine, it ended up in success. Stirred by a passion for pastries, and meeting a need to create beautiful cakes for her children's birthdays, Kristen's cakes took shape.

Family and friends began to ask for cakes for their special events, and so the creative outlet continued to be fed. Later, Kristen mixed in royal icing cookies and loves the edible art made with each custom piece.
